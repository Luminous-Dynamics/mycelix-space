## Governance Innovation

### Space Law 2.0

**Problem**: Current space law (Outer Space Treaty 1967) is inadequate for commercial space.

**Solution**: Decentralized governance that creates de facto standards.

#### Key Principles
1. **Common Heritage** - Resources belong to humanity, but extractors get rewarded
2. **First-Mover Rights** - Discovery and investment create legitimate claims
3. **Use It or Lose It** - Prevent speculative squatting
4. **Safety First** - Operations must not endanger others
5. **Environmental Stewardship** - Minimize debris and contamination

#### Implementation
- **Reputation System**: Operators earn trust through safe operations
- **Graduated Permissions**: New entrants start with limited rights
- **Community Arbitration**: Disputes resolved by staked validators
- **Treaty Compatibility**: Designed to work within (not replace) international law
